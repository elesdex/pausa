# Product vision

## One sentence

Pausa is a calm, accessible help button for moments of digital uncertainty, starting with suspicious messages and scam prevention.

## Problem

Scams succeed partly by creating urgency, fear, authority, or confusion before a person has time to verify. Existing tools often begin too late: they expect the person to recognize a scam category, know how to capture the evidence, or interpret a technical verdict.

People with less confidence using technology are especially underserved, but the vulnerable moment is universal.

## Promise

Pausa helps a person stop, share what they see in the easiest available way, understand the warning signals, and take one safer next step.

## Primary audience

For the MVP: adults who receive suspicious SMS, WhatsApp, email, website, or phone-related messages and want simple guidance. The interface is deliberately accessible to older adults and people with limited technical experience without branding them as incapable.

## Core experience

1. A suspicious message creates fear or urgency.
2. The person opens Pausa from their phone home screen.
3. Pausa immediately asks them not to respond or click yet.
4. The person pastes text, dictates, takes a photo, or uploads a screenshot.
5. GPT-5.6 identifies signals and uncertainty.
6. Pausa explains the risk in plain language.
7. Pausa recommends an independently verifiable next step.
8. The person can listen to the guidance aloud and learn one reusable pattern.

## MVP boundary

Pausa does not:

- monitor WhatsApp, SMS, email, calls, or the screen in the background;
- promise that content is definitely safe or fraudulent;
- contact emergency services, banks, police, or family automatically;
- store uploaded content or create user accounts;
- provide financial, legal, medical, or emergency advice;
- support every emergency scenario in the hackathon version.

## Future vision

The product can grow into a personal “help me” router for other moments of uncertainty, with locally appropriate and user-configured trusted contacts. That broader vision remains narrative only until the anti-scam module is reliable and safe.

## Success for the hackathon

A judge can understand the problem in 10 seconds, complete the end-to-end demo in under 60 seconds, see meaningful GPT-5.6 vision and reasoning, and understand exactly how Codex helped a non-programmer turn a voice-developed idea into working open-source software.
