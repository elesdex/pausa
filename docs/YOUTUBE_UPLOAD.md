## Description

Someone in my family was scammed by a message that looked real and felt urgent. I built Pausa for that moment.

Pausa is a free, open-source, bilingual web app that helps families slow down before responding to a suspicious digital message. A person can speak, take a photo, choose a screenshot, or paste the message. Pausa uses GPT-5.6 to explain observable warning signs and recommend one calmer, safer next step.

Try Pausa:
https://pausa-digital.elesdex.chatgpt.site/

Source code:
https://github.com/elesdex/pausa

Built for OpenAI Build Week with Codex and GPT-5.6.

Codex helped turn a voice conversation about a real family problem into a working bilingual product: product strategy, interaction design, implementation, safety rules, evaluations, testing, documentation, and deployment. GPT-5.6 provides the live reasoning and vision used to analyze voluntarily shared text and images.

Pausa requires no account, performs no background monitoring, includes no analytics, and stores no submissions in its own database. It provides guidance, not a guarantee, and always directs verification away from suspicious links, phone numbers, and QR codes.
